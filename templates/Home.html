Multiobjective Portfolio Optimization App

In this web app, the portfolio optimization is formulated and solved as a multiobjective optimization problem. Expected volitility and expected return are considered as conflicting objectives of the multi-objective problem and the non-dominated sorting genetic algorithm (NSGA-II) is used for optimization.

Please note that for each stock in the portfolio, only previous 365 days of prices are used as historical data.


Disclaimer: this web app is developed for research purposes only and shall Not be used for trading or providing recommendations.
